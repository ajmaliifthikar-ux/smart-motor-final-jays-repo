### Phase 2: Ultimate Site Architecture & URL Taxonomy

Your Next.js dynamic routing must be flat, logical, and designed to hoist link equity to high-value pages. **Do not use sub-domains.**

#### **1. Master Slug Strategy**

Use hyphens, lowercase, and no filler words (stop words).

* **Bad:** `/services/we-do-mercedes-repair`
* **Elite:** `/services/mercedes-repair`

#### **2. The Hierarchy (Parent-Child Map)**

* `smartmotor.ae/brands/porsche` (Target: "Porsche Service Center Abu Dhabi")
* `smartmotor.ae/brands/mercedes-benz` (Target: "Mercedes Garage Musaffah")
* *Critical:* These pages must link *internally* to specific services. (e.g., "Book your **Porsche Oil Change**" -> links to Oil Change service).


* **Location Landing Pages (The Local Capture):**
* `smartmotor.ae/locations/musaffah-m9-auto-repair`
* *Why:* To capture "mechanic near me" searches specifically in your industrial zone.



#### **3. Cannibalization Prevention**

* **The Risk:** `/services/brake-repair` vs. `/brands/porsche` (optimizing for "Porsche Brake Repair").
* **The Fix:**
* The **Service Page** targets the *functional* keyword: "Brake Pad Replacement Abu Dhabi," "Ceramic Brake Discs."
* The **Brand Page** targets the *nominal* keyword: "Porsche Maintenance," "Mercedes B Service."
* **Canonical Rule:** If a user searches "Porsche Brake Repair," the **Brand Page** should be the primary ranker. Structure the Brand page with a section called "Common Porsche Repairs" that links to the generic Brake service page.

### Phase 4: Local Authority & Technical Action Steps

#### **1. Local SEO Dominance: Schema Injection**

You must speak Google's language. Inject this JSON-LD schema into your `layout.tsx` or individual page heads.

* **`LocalBusiness` + `AutoRepair`:** Define your `priceRange` as "$$$" (signals luxury). Define `openingHours` strictly.
* **`Service` Schema:** On every Service Page, nest the specific service type.
* *Example:* On the Oil Change page, use `AutoOilChange` type.


* **`Brand` Schema:** On Brand pages, explicitly link your service center to the `brand` entity (e.g., "specializes in Porsche").
* **`FAQPage` Schema:** On the Homepage and Services page, wrap your Q&A section in schema. This wins you the "People Also Ask" snippets on Google.

#### **2. 30-Day Go-Live Checklist**

1. **Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization:** Claim the listing for "M9 Musaffah." Upload 20+ photos of *high-end cars* on lifts. Rename services in GBP from "Auto Repair" to "Porsche Repair," "Ceramic Coating," etc.
2. **The "Local Stack" Footer:** Ensure your footer address is not just text, but a clickable Google Maps embed link. Format it exactly: `Smart Motor, M9, Musaffah Industrial Area, Abu Dhabi`.
3. **Speed Optimization (Core Web Vitals):** Luxury sites often fail here due to heavy images. Use **Next.js Image Optimization** (`next/image`) with WebP format. Ensure your "Hero Video" has a lightweight poster image for instant loading.
4. **Citation Building:** Register Smart Motor on **YallaMotor**, **Dubizzle Cars**, and **2GIS**. These are high-authority local directories that validate your existence to Google.
5. **Canonical Tag Audit:** Ensure every single page has a `rel="canonical"` tag pointing to itself (or its master page) to prevent duplicate content issues with the dynamic filters.
